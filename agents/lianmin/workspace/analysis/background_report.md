# Background Report: Lianmin Zheng

## Biography Synthesis

### Early Education & Foundations
Lianmin Zheng completed his B.S. at ACM Honored Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University—a prestigious program known for nurturing top CS talent in China. This foundation prepared him for advanced research in systems and ML.

### Ph.D. Research & Mentorship
- **Institution**: UC Berkeley
- **Advisors**: Ion Stoica (renowned systems researcher, co-founder of Databricks) and Joseph E. Gonzalez (ML systems expert)
- **Research Focus**: Machine learning systems, particularly for training and serving large models
- **Key Publications**: Papers at OSDI, NeurIPS, ICML on distributed training and serving systems

### Career Progression
1. **Member of Technical Staff at xAI**: Leading the inference team responsible for building infrastructure to run Grok models. This is a critical role at one of the most ambitious AI companies.

2. **Ph.D. at UC Berkeley**: Developed foundational work on ML systems including Alpa (distributed training), FlexGen (high-throughput inference), and contributed to vLLM.

3. **Co-founder of LMSYS.org**: Built a non-profit organization that has become incredibly influential in the open LLM space, creating Vicuna models and Chatbot Arena.

### Research Evolution
Lianmin's work has evolved from deep learning compilers (TVM/Ansor) to full-stack ML systems for large models. His trajectory shows a keen sense for identifying bottlenecks in emerging AI systems and building solutions that are both research contributions and widely-used open-source tools.

### Key Collaborations
- **Ion Stoica & Joseph Gonzalez**: Ph.D. advisors who shaped his systems thinking
- **LMSYS Team**: Collaborators on open LLM initiatives
- **xAI Team**: Working on production-scale inference infrastructure

### Impact & Influence
- **Academic**: Papers at top venues (OSDI, NeurIPS, ICML, MLSys)
- **Industry**: Systems like vLLM, SGLang, and FastChat are widely adopted
- **Open Source**: Projects with millions of downloads and users
- **Community**: Chatbot Arena has become a standard evaluation platform

### Personal Style & Philosophy
Lianmin's work demonstrates:
- **Practical impact**: Building systems that solve real problems at scale
- **Open source commitment**: Making research accessible through open tools
- **Systematic approach**: Moving from compilers to distributed systems to full-stack LLM infrastructure
- **Collaborative spirit**: Co-founding non-profits and working across institutions

## Summary
Lianmin Zheng is a rising star in ML systems research, bridging academic innovation with industrial impact. His work spans the entire stack from low-level compiler optimization to large-scale distributed inference systems. At xAI, he's applying this expertise to build infrastructure for some of the largest AI models in production. His open-source contributions through LMSYS.org have democratized access to LLM evaluation and fine-tuning tools.
